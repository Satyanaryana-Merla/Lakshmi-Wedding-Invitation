The seven steps or vows (Saptapadi) taken during a Hindu marriage ceremony are:

  1. First Step (Pratham Charan): Commitment to nourish each other, share responsibilities, and provide for the well-being of the family.
  2. Second Step (Dvitiya Charan): Pledge to develop physical, mental, and spiritual strength together.
  3. Third Step (Tritiya Charan): Vow to prosper and increase wealth through righteous means.
  4. Fourth Step (Chaturthi Charan): Promise to acquire knowledge, happiness, and harmony.
  5. Fifth Step (Panchami Charan): Commitment to raise strong and virtuous children.
  6. Sixth Step (Shashthi Charan): Resolve to enjoy all seasons of life together.
  7. Seventh Step (Saptami Charan): Dedication to be lifelong companions, true to each other, and loyal in all circumstances.
